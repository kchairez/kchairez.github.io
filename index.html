ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

-Kevin Chairez

This website was created in order to give individuals the basis on growing the most common and easiest produce. This will give individuals the experience to learn an essential life skill, which is how to learn to grow edible plants. Growing these basic plants will allow individuals to obtain the basic skills and knowledge to further extend their plant variety, beyond these plants. All of the plants shown on the website are plants that I have found to be the easiest and most delicious to grow.

This website is for an educational personal purposes for Purdue University. All copyright images are used for educational purposes.
